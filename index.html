      Help Centre

      Detailed guidance on utilizing this platform and its built-in accessibility features.

      Accessibility Quick-Start Guide

      GlobalTech is committed to creating an inclusive workplace. This platform is designed to be fully navigable via keyboard and compatible with popular screen readers like JAWS, NVDA, and VoiceOver.

      Contrast Options

      Toggle between Light, Dark, and High Contrast theme modes in the Accessibility Center (top right menu, or Alt + A). High Contrast mode meets WCAG AAA guidelines for color brightness and text readability.

      Dyslexia-Friendly Layout

      When activated, the layout alters baseline line-spacing, expands word spacing, and loads a dyslexia-adapted sans-serif typeface to assist reading accuracy.

      Reading Ruler Overlay

      Use the Reading Guide ruler to superimpose a transparent highlighting bar that tracks your cursor. This aids focus and simplifies reading line-by-line in long documents.

      Visual Audio Alerts

      If you have hearing impairments, toggling "Sound Visual Equivalents" will output visual toast flashes whenever system notification chimes are triggered.

      For custom assistance, please contact the Accessibility Services team at inclusion@globaltech.com.
